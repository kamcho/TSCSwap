Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa payments for all our plans. You'll receive a payment request on your phone when you subscribe.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Your subscription will be prorated based on the remaining term.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! You can start with our free plan to explore the platform. No credit card required.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. There are no cancellation fees, but no refunds are provided for the unused portion of the annual subscription.
