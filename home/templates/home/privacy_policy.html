1. Introduction

Welcome to TSC Swap ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our platform to find teacher swap partners.

Important: TSC Swap is an independent organization that helps teachers find swap partners. We are not affiliated with the Teachers Service Commission (TSC). We do not share your personal information with third parties, and we delete your data upon request.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide our services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, TSC number (if provided)
  • Profile Information: Teaching level, subjects taught, current school information, location preferences
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through our platform, WhatsApp messages (if you use our WhatsApp bot), and chat history
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our platform, including swap requests, preferences, and search history
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and access logs

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • To match you with potential swap partners based on your preferences and requirements
  • To facilitate communication between teachers interested in swapping
  • To provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • To improve our platform and services
  • To send you important updates about your account and swap requests
  • To ensure platform security and prevent fraud or abuse

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure database storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information in your profile
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from non-essential communications

7. WhatsApp Business API

If you use our WhatsApp bot, please note:

  • Your messages are processed through Meta's WhatsApp Business API
  • We store your queries and our responses to improve our services
  • Your phone number is used solely to identify you and provide personalized responses
  • We do not share your WhatsApp conversations with third parties
  • You can opt out of WhatsApp communications at any time

For more information about Meta's data practices, please review their Privacy Policy.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze platform usage and improve our services

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our platform.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as resolving disputes or enforcing agreements).

10. Children's Privacy

Our platform is intended for teachers and educational professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
